Overview

As a student, I pursued a challenging academic path. I began my higher education journey in "classes préparatoires aux grandes écoles", a two-year program designed to prepare students for admission to prestigious engineering schools. I then pursued my studies in engineering at École Centrale de Lyon, where I gained a solid foundation in various engineering disciplines, especially in computer science and mathematics. As a double curriculum student, I also studied for a bachelor's degree in mathematics at Claude Bernard University, expanding my knowledge in different fields of mathematics. My academic path has equipped me with a rigorous and diverse skill set, as well as the ability to excel in complex and demanding environments.
